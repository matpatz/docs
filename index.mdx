The documination contains information sourced, and copied from the [Potassium](https://docs.potassium.pro/api-reference/introduction) documentation. Thanks.

Before you continue, the documentation assumes that you are already familiar with [**Luau**](https://luau.org/) and basic programming.

The environment follows the [**UNC**](https://github.com/unified-naming-convention/NamingStandard) standard, with lots of tweaks and additions.

Alongside every function and feature, there is always an example to make sure you understand how to use them.

The documentation will be kept up to date and is subject to change.
