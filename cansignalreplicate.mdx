```luau
cansignalreplicate(signal: RBXScriptSignal): boolean
```

That covers `RemoteEvent.OnServerEvent{:luau}`, `RemoteEvent.OnClientEvent{:luau}`, `BindableEvent.Event{:luau}`, `Folder.ChildAdded{:luau}`, and the rest.

Most commonly used to confirm a value is a valid `RBXScriptSignal{:luau}` before passing it to `firesignal{:luau}` or other signal functions.
